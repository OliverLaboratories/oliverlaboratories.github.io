Carlos Oliver Homepage

I am postdoctoral researcher at the Machine Learning and Computational Biology Lab at ETH Zurich, based in Basel, Switzerland. I was a member of the labs of Jérôme Waldispühl and William L. Hamilton during my PhD studies in Computer Science.

I build algorithms that help us understand network data (often biological).
