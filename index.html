Carlos Oliver Homepage

I am a project leader at the Department of Machine Learning and Systems Biology at the Max Planck Institute of Biochemistry , based in Martinsried, Germany. Formerly postdoc at ETH Zurich and a member of the labs of Jérôme Waldispühl and William L. Hamilton during my PhD studies in Computer Science.

I build algorithms that help us understand network data (often biological).
