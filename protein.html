Carlos & Victoria's Protein Calculator




How does it work?

Ever find yourself spending hours at the grocery store staring at the nutritional information of 10 different yoghurts looking for the one that will give you the most protein for the fewest calories and lowest cost? Look no further. Simply enter basic nutritional info to get a score for calorie and cost efficiency of any food with respect to its protein content. To compare different products just click 'Another one!' and enter the new item's information. A table at the bottom of the page will hold all the results.
